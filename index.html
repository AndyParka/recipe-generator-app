How to Use Recipe Generator

Step 1: Add Your Ingredients

Add any ingredients you remember having in your fridge or pantry. Don't worry about listing everything - just the main items you want to use.

Examples: capsicum, chicken, stock, garlic, onions, rice, tomatoes, etc.

Step 2: Describe What You're Craving

Tell us what you're in the mood for. Be as general or specific as you like!

Examples:

  • "A comfy warm soup that's easy"
  • "Something for meal prep"
  • "Quick and healthy dinner"
  • "Spicy Asian-inspired dish"
  • "Light lunch with fresh flavors"

Step 3: Generate & Choose

Press "Generate Recipes" and pick what you're going to cook! Save your favorites for later.
